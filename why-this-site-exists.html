Why this site exists

July 2026

Everything I've written so far lives on platforms I don't control. This site is the correction: one address, owned by me, where my work accumulates for as long as I keep making it.

It's deliberately plain — black text on white, a few photographs, nothing else. The websites I admire most are like this. They've looked roughly the same for fifteen years, and they'll look the same in fifteen more, because there's nothing in them that can go out of date except the ideas.

That's the bet I'm making here. Not that everything I publish will be good, but that having one permanent place to put it will make me more honest about what I think.
