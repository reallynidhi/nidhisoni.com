Hi, I'm Nidhi.

I live in Bangalore. I created this website to give a permanent home to everything I make — essays, projects, photographs, and whatever comes next.

Until recently I built and ran investor relations at PL Capital, working across a crypto fund and a neurotech fund. I also was proudly a part of 2 person team running PLVS — a Venture Studio that partnered with founders at idea stage to give their startup a shape. I have hosted several events across the world, I love connecting with people and learning from them. Before that, an engineering degree in computer science taught me how things are built; the years since have been about learning why some of them matter.

Right now I'm in a deliberate period of deep study — mostly space and deep tech — trying to find a direction worth twenty years, not two. You can see what that looks like day to day on the now page.

I come from a family of goldsmiths in Madhya Pradesh, lovingly brought up in Gujarat which is probably why I can't stop thinking about craft: things made carefully, by hand, meant to last.
